u_int16_t pacpus::ScanHeader::scannerStatus

Status of the scanner.

  • 0x0007: reserved,
  • 0x0008: set frequency reached,
  • 0x0010: external sync signal detected,
  • 0x0020: sync ok,
  • 0x0040: sync master (instead of slave),
  • 0xFF80: reserved
